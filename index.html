Photography

My photography focuses on post-production that will push the image in a direction that extracts the feel of the original photo. If the photograph is composed in some fun way, I will aim to make it more playful. If the picture is gritty, I use editing to make it feel rougher. I think you get the idea.

Bee Decline

A group project where we researched the cause of bee population decline and presented information that would raise awareness about the issue. My role in this project was to take the research and come up with how we would present the information to multiple audiences through the means of a website.
