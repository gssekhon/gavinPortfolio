Bee Population Decline in Ontario

Problem

The problem presented to our group was that we needed to display facts and information about the bee population decline that has been occurring in recent years, specifically, in Ontario, Canada. We were to taken an unbiased stance on the situation and simply present the problems, viable solutions, and present it to the target audience.

We researched the topic and came up all the information needed, and then we took it and needed to design a way for it to be communicated on the web. This is where the majority of my work came in.

Mockups

Our main audience is the lay person, as one of the main solutions we identified relies on the awareness of the general public. Our goal is to spread awareness on how to save the bees, and most people are not aware of the problem at is plaguing society and how they can help.

Final

From here, we finalized out final website and incorporated the research to make a parallax website that could display the information to a lay audience without seeming like a boring government website.

The parallax design of the website makes it feel as if the bees in the background are flying upwards on the screen. The design and layout stays static throughout scrolling on the page. This keeps it uniform and easier for a lay person to read, while adding enough flair to keep them interested.
